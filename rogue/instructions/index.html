Instructions

You are "P"

Use these buttons below to move your character


Below these buttons are SUPER movements (They move you 3 times at once and are for convenience)


Scattered throughout the levels are "M" (monsters) Upon getting within one tile of these, combat mode will start

Also scattered through the levels are "H" (health packs) These increase your life by one

Finally, in each level are "E" (exits) These advance you to further levels

Your goal is to get to the highest possible level

Combat Guide

Upon entering combat, you will be greeted with "0123456789"

A cursor will scroll through these numbers

Press down on "ATTACK!!" whenever you want and try to release it on the highest possible number

If you hit, the enemy will lose the corresponding amount of health

If you miss, you lose a life

The game ends if you die 3 times, so keep an eye on that life counter and try not to get into any uneccesary battles
